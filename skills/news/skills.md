# News Research Skill
Searches for and analyzes market news from various sources.

## Tools
- `search_market_news(query)`: Search for general market news.
- `search_yahoo_finance_news(query)`: Search specifically on Yahoo Finance.
- `get_sector_sentiment(sector)`: Analyze sentiment for a specific sector.
- `analyze_news_impact(news_text)`: Extract stock impacts from news snippets.
